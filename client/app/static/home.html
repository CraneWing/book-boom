Welcome to BookBOOM

The Web's Trading Post for Books!

This is a site for those with a passion for reading and the simple joy of sitting down with a good, old-fashioned, paper and print, honest-to-goodness, book. Nothing digital on here but this site.

By signing up for an account, you can browse books people want to trade — paperback and hardcover — or post volumes you'd like to trade.

You can contact other users who have the titles your want (whether around the corner or across the continent) to arrange a trade. You get what you want, and vice versa for them Our desired end result is happy readers from coast to coast!

So, go on to the next chapter (pun intended!) and sign up for an account at BookBOOM today!
